Questions

Before you start hunting

Is Hunter Pro financial advice? +
No. Signals are informational and educational tools reflecting our engine's analysis. You decide whether, when, and how much to trade — always at your own risk.
Which markets and brokers does it work with? +
Signals cover major forex pairs, top crypto assets, and large-cap equities. Since alerts are informational, they work alongside any broker or exchange you already use.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — all plans are month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel from your account settings and you won't be billed again.
How is the win rate calculated? +
We track every signal from alert to outcome and log whether target or stop was hit first, across a rolling 12-month window. Methodology notes are available in your dashboard.
